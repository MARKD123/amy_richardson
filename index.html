Amy Richardson

Direct Access Barrister


I was called to the bar of England and Wales 2009 and have over 12 years advocacy experience and client management in a variety of areas, namely, childcare (both public and private), high value ancillary relief, divorce and non-molestation orders (both for applicant and respondent) I have worked offshore and am a qualified Guernsey Advocate in addition to being a member of the Bar of England and Wales. I was also a partner within a busy offshore family firm. Due to this, I have been fortunate enough to have exposure to an extremely broad and high-calibre scope of work, and so can operate a varied and high-volume practice. Since returning to the United Kingdom, I have worked in house for a Local Authority within their children safeguarding department with a specialism in the conduct of cases with allegation of serious sexual abuse and neglect.
